Our story

The shop with a vitamin factory behind it

Good & Green opened in 2016 as the retail home of G&G Vitamins — a family-run East Grinstead company that has been making encapsulated vitamins for over 50 years and ships them to more than 50 countries. In other words: the people selling you supplements here actually make them.

Founded by Lizzie Calcioli — as organic and local as possible, from day one
1,200+ product lines packed into one friendly shop on London Road
Sister store in Tunbridge Wells — with Emilio's coffee shop upstairs
